Practice Sites

  • Hack The Box
    • Hack The Box is a great site for people who already have a working knowledge of penetration testing. It provides lots of boxes of varying difficulty with no hand-holding. While a box is active posting walkthroughs are against the terms of service and can get you banned so you are on your own outside of hints on the forums.
  • Try Hack Me
    • This is a newer site but has started to gain popularity quite quickly. It is a little more beginner focused than Hack The Box is but that doesn't mean that there isn't a lot of difficult challenges here because there is.
  • VulnHub
    • VulnHub allows you to download intentionally vulnerable virtual machines that you can test your skills against. Again machines here can vary across the difficulty spectrum.
